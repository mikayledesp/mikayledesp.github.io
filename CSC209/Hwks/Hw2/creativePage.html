London Pros
  • The tube is a really effective and fast way to get around the city.

  • The Indian and Korean Food there was great ! I also loved the fish and chips too.

  • Absolutely beautiful architecture.
Cons
  • Everything is quite pricy.

  • The city can be isolating if you go alone.

New York City Pros
  • The subway is 24 hours meaning you can adventure at all ours of the night!

  • NYC being such a diverse place means that they have a wide array of different meals from all over the world!

  • Museuems and the NYC Skyline are a must see!
Cons
  • Everything is expensive, especially in Manhattan.

  • The subway is very dirty compared to transportation in Europe or Asia.
 10/10 (extremnely biased as I live there)
